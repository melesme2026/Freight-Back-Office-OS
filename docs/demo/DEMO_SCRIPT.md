# Freight Back Office OS Demo Script (5–7 Minutes)

## Audience
- Owner-operators
- Dispatchers / back-office operators
- Teams currently managing paperwork through email + WhatsApp + spreadsheets

## Demo Goal
Show one complete story: **load created → documents collected → invoice + packet prepared → broker/factoring submission ready → payment tracked**.

## Talk Track Structure
- **Minute 0:00–0:45 — Problem setup**
- **Minute 0:45–2:15 — Core workflow walkthrough (load to packet)**
- **Minute 2:15–3:30 — Follow-up + payment visibility**
- **Minute 3:30–4:30 — Driver portal + collaboration**
- **Minute 4:30–5:30 — Dashboard + operational control**
- **Minute 5:30–7:00 — Pricing + pilot offer + close**

---

## Screen-by-Screen Script

### 1) Landing / Opening (0:00–0:45)
**Screen:** Home + pricing CTA.

**Voiceover:**
> "Most small trucking businesses don’t lose money because they can’t haul loads. They lose time and cash because paperwork and follow-up are scattered across chats, emails, and spreadsheets. Freight Back Office OS brings the entire post-booking workflow into one system so you can submit faster and get paid with fewer delays."

**Key points to say:**
- We are not replacing dispatch booking marketplaces.
- We are focused on what happens *after* load booking.
- Primary outcome: faster and cleaner payment operations.

---

### 2) Load Creation (0:45–1:30)
**Screen:** Dashboard → Loads → New Load.

**Voiceover:**
> "Every workflow starts from a load record with broker, customer, driver, and financial details captured in one place."

**Key points to say:**
- Load record becomes the operational source of truth.
- Team does not need to hunt through inbox threads.
- Better start = fewer invoice/factoring mistakes later.

---

### 3) Document Intake & Review (1:30–2:15)
**Screen:** Documents + load detail packet readiness.

**Voiceover:**
> "As documents arrive from email, uploads, or driver portal, we tie them to the load and track readiness for invoicing and submission."

**Key points to say:**
- Show BOL/POD/rate confirmation/invoice expectations.
- Highlight missing-doc visibility.
- Emphasize reduced back-and-forth.

---

### 4) Invoice + Submission Packet (2:15–3:00)
**Screen:** Load detail invoice/packet section.

**Voiceover:**
> "Once required docs are ready, staff can prepare the invoice and packet for broker or factoring submission with a consistent process."

**Key points to say:**
- Submission quality drives payment speed.
- Standardized packet reduces rejection risk.
- Team can prove what was submitted and when.

---

### 5) Email Follow-Up + Payment Tracking (3:00–3:30)
**Screen:** Follow-ups + money/payment dashboard.

**Voiceover:**
> "After submission, we track follow-up tasks and payment status, so nothing gets stuck in ‘sent but forgotten.’"

**Key points to say:**
- Visibility into submitted, funded, paid stages.
- Follow-up cadence can be operationalized.
- Better cash predictability for owner-operators.

---

### 6) Driver Portal (3:30–4:30)
**Screen:** Driver portal (loads/uploads/support/billing).

**Voiceover:**
> "Drivers can upload paperwork and check load and billing visibility without needing full staff dashboard access."

**Key points to say:**
- Reduces dispatcher/admin bottlenecks.
- Keeps driver and office aligned.
- Useful for teams transitioning from purely manual document handoff.

---

### 7) Dashboard Control Layer (4:30–5:30)
**Screen:** Main dashboard + review queue + onboarding checklist.

**Voiceover:**
> "Operations can see what needs attention now—missing docs, review queue, and onboarding readiness—so work gets done in priority order."

**Key points to say:**
- Centralized queue-based operations.
- Easier training for back-office staff.
- Helps teams scale beyond one dispatcher doing everything manually.

---

### 8) Pricing + Pilot Close (5:30–7:00)
**Screen:** Pricing page + request demo.

**Voiceover:**
> "We currently offer Starter and Growth plans designed for owner-led and growing teams. Pilot onboarding includes workflow setup support so your first week is productive."

**Close CTA:**
- "Start trial" for ready users.
- "Request demo" for guided setup.
- "Login" for invited teams.
- Optional talking point: "Most small fleets win first by tightening packet quality and follow-up, then adding APIs when submission volume justifies it."

---

## Persona-Specific Pitch Variants

### Owner-Operator
- Focus: faster paperwork completion and cash timing visibility.
- Line: "You keep hauling; we help keep your payment process organized and on-time."

### Dispatcher / Back-Office Operator
- Focus: fewer manual reminders, fewer packet errors, clearer priorities.
- Line: "This gives you one queue instead of five disconnected channels."

### Manual Paperwork User
- Focus: transition path from chat/email folders to structured workflow.
- Line: "You can keep your current intake channels while improving internal process discipline."

## Demo Delivery Tips
- Use one real-like load example end-to-end.
- Avoid overpromising AI or integrations not yet in production.
- Keep language operational and practical (not technical jargon heavy).
- Integration positioning: for pilot users, emphasize **email/portal packet workflows first**; present DAT/Truckstop/factoring APIs as **later phase after volume validation**.
