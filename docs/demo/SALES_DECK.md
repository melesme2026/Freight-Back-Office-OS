---

## Slide 2 — Problem
**Today’s reality for many trucking teams**

- Load docs scattered across WhatsApp, email, and phones
- Missing BOL/POD delays invoice submission
- Broker/factoring follow-up is manual and inconsistent
- Owner has weak visibility into what is submitted vs paid
- Payment delays hurt cash flow and planning

**Speaker note:** Pain is not hauling loads; pain is post-booking paperwork and payment ops.

---

## Slide 3 — Solution
**A single operating system for post-booking back office**

- Load-centered operational record
- Centralized document collection and tracking
- Invoice + packet readiness workflow
- Follow-up and payment status visibility
- Staff/driver role separation for better execution

---

## Slide 4 — Workflow Slide (Core Engine)
**Load → Docs → Invoice → Packet → Payment**

1. Create load with broker/customer/driver details
2. Collect and link required documents
3. Confirm invoice readiness
4. Prepare and submit packet
5. Track funding/payment and follow-ups

**Speaker note:** This is the core value chain and should stay stable.

---

## Slide 5 — Driver Portal
**Driver collaboration without dashboard overload**

- Driver login for assigned load visibility
- Upload docs directly
- Support ticket access
- Billing visibility (role-scoped)

**Value:** reduces office bottlenecks and status confusion.

---

## Slide 6 — Invoice + Submission Packet
**Submission quality = payment speed**

- Standard packet flow helps reduce missing attachments
- Better submission traceability for broker/factoring follow-up
- Supports repeatable process for staff, not one-person tribal knowledge

---

## Slide 7 — Money Dashboard
**Operational cash visibility**

- Track submitted/follow-up/paid stages
- Review aging and outstanding payment work
- Identify stuck loads requiring intervention

**Speaker note:** Position as operational control, not accounting replacement.

## Slide 10 — Integration Roadmap (Reality-Based)
**How integration actually scales**

- Phase 1: Manual/email + portal submission discipline (today)
- Phase 2: Submission evidence + portal tracking
- Phase 3: Selective API integrations (DAT/Truckstop first, then high-impact factoring/payment endpoints)

**Speaker note:** Don’t overpromise "full integrations" in early pilot. Prove workflow value first, then automate high-volume lanes.
